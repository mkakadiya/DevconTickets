Blockchain base Devcon conference tickets


- Ticket Purchase with Ether
	- Send ether to contract
	- Validate ticket amount and issue a ticket
	- Contract Issues tickets until event date or it's sold out 
	
- Refund/Cancellation 
	- Refund purchased ticket amount
	- Refund can be issued in 24 hour of the purchase time

- Contract Owner can withdraw amount
- Withdrawal can be done after event completed
